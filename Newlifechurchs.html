Message from Rev

Samuel Kamathi

“

Welcome to Newlife Foundation Church

Dear beloved, It is with great joy and heartfelt anticipation that I, Rev Samuel Kamathi, welcome you to Newlife Foundation Church. We continue to stand firm in the transformative power of faith and the boundless love of our Savior, Jesus Christ.

As the Visionary Leader of this vibrant and thriving ministry, I extend my hand in friendship and my heart in love to you. Therefore, I take this opportunity to welcome you to Newlife Foundation Church.

Whether you are seeking answers or simply a place to belong, know that you are valued, you are loved, and you are welcomed with joy. It is our deepest desire to see lives changed and hearts transformed by the timeless truths of God's Word.

Whether you are joining us for the first time or returning home, may you feel embraced by a community that is committed to walking alongside you in your spiritual journey. Welcome home to Newlife Foundation Church—where every soul is cherished and every heart finds refuge.

”
